Internal Power Shifts

Legal and compliance narratives became dominant

  • Champion Power Decay: The value champion lost influence from Round 5 onward as implementation-speed concerns weakened the case.
  • Dominant Narrative: Legal and compliance agents controlled the negotiation frame.
  • Trust Decay: Institutional trust remained at 1.0, so supplier confidence did not materially erode.
Talk-track and Advices

Best next move for a renewed attempt

  • Narrative Adjustment: Stop emphasizing implementation speed and pivot toward liability risk mitigation.
  • Concession Strategy: Hold firm on scope rigidity (0.55) and payment terms (18 days), while moving the liability cap toward the 2.0x range.
  • Failed Assumption: The seller overestimated buyer tolerance for a 2.5x liability cap; legal's 1.0x redline remained non-negotiable.
  • Critical Fix: Package a lower liability cap with extended contract duration (3.2+ years) to create reciprocal value.
